TimeBot

by Ray Zheng

Hi! I’m Ray Zheng, a third-year Computer Science student at Northeastern University. I built TimeBot as a personal project to help teams and communities organize their schedules more efficiently.

Many people rely on Discord to manage events, clubs, and practices, but keeping track of everything across channels and servers quickly becomes messy. TimeBot integrates directly with Google Calendar, allowing users to schedule events, submit availability, and receive reminders — all without ever leaving Discord.

What started as a tool for varsity esports scheduling has grown into a flexible system for any group that needs clean, organized time management inside their server.
